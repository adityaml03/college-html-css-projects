Padme: A New Superheroine Rises on the Big Screen

Movie News | Published: Sept 9, 2025 | By CineWorld News Agency

Kochi: The much-awaited superhero film Padme has caught the attention of audiences worldwide. The movie introduces a bold and powerful heroine who rises to defend humanity in the midst of chaos and destruction. With stunning visuals and intense storytelling, Padme promises to redefine the superhero genre in Indian cinema. Directed by Abraham Qureshi, produced by Lucifer, and starring Adithi Roy, the film showcases a gripping tale of courage, sacrifice, and destiny.
